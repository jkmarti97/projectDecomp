## SAFe Definitions
- **Portfolio Epic:**
  A large, strategic initiative spanning **multiple Agile Release Trains (ARTs)** or **value streams**.
  It aligns with **portfolio objectives**, requires **significant investment**, and typically spans **more than 6 months**.

- **Feature:**
  A capability or service that provides measurable value to a **customer or stakeholder**, delivered by **one ART** within a **single Program Increment (PI)** (typically 8–12 weeks).

- **Story:**
  A small, implementable unit of work that delivers **direct, testable value**, completed by a **single Agile team** within **one to two sprints** (1–4 weeks).
